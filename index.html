FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What type of platform is H7?

H7 is a hybrid education platform. We combine online and technology-based methods to enhance the learning process.

Who is H7 for?

This platform is designed for students from KG to Grade 12, offering both academic and skill-based education.

What can you learn through H7 EdTech?

Core subjects such as English, Math, Science and Malayalam. Also creative skills like music, craft, and abacus.

Does the platform include parent support?

Parents can track their child's academic performance through report generation, monitor assignments, and get alerts.
